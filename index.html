About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in Numerical Geosciences / Applied Mathematics at Johannes Gutenberg University Mainz. My research interests are the analysis and numerics for partial differential equations coming from geological applications. Within this field my main focus lies on space-time adaptive methods for viscoelastic flow models.
